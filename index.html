About the Conference

The beautiful city of Salt Lake City will be the host city for the 2017 Better Workplace Conference!

For 20 years, this event has been the premier forum for introducing and exploring concrete actions you can take to improve your workplace.

Expert Speakers

Our expert speaker lineup was just announced, so don't wait too long before grabbing your tickets!

Gain perspective on the workplace of the future from the leaders who are building it. You will learn practical strategies to improve how your leaders lead, and to better the experience of your employees and your customers, built on a strong foundation of better wellness for your organization.

The Better Workplace Bureau (BWB) is committed to being inclusive and welcoming for everyone. We look forward to another intensive day of learning and sharing.

What You'll Learn

How to Create a Wellness Culture Building and Leading a Diverse and Inclusive Workforce Improving through Evaluating and Acting on Contact Evaluation Building Great Cultures for a Better Experience How a New Generation of Leadership Redefines the Paradigm

Speakers

Madison Louis

Madison is an senior officer for the Mental Health Commission (MHC) and is committed to changing attitudes and behaviors toward mental health issues in the workplace.

Janet Michaels

Janet is a financial services manager who aims to provide a diverse range of insurance, wealth and asset management solutions to individuals and corporate clients.

Raymond Meyers

Raymond is an human resources coordinator in the public sector with expertise and extensive work experience in government, higher education and health care.

Laurie Samuels

Laurie is a safety operations conductor and offers safety services such as alcohol and drug testing, fatigue management, occupational health and injury management programs.

Terry Sanders

Terry is a partner at the Courage Group and works with businesses to implement strategies that shape positive work cultures and provide a sense of context, purpose, clarity and security.

Mason Reynolds

Mason is a diversity officer who advocates for new forms of organization and works to reinvent of workplace cultures to encourage innovation and increased participation.

About the Better Workplace Bureau

BWB does not focus on who provides the best benefits package or who has the highest employee engagement scores. BWB is committed to sharing experiences and learning from seasoned leaders and employers who are committed to making better workplaces.
